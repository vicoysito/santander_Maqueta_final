FAQ
  • Why do I need to register?
    By registering, you can earn some really cool rewards just for enjoying your favorite drinks!
  • How do I join Club Jarritos and create an account?
    o The “Register Here” link is located in the upper right hand corner of the screen and throughout the site. Simply fill out the form and you’re in the club.
  • Who is eligible?
    The program is open to legal residents of the contiguous United States and the District of Columbia (residents of AK, HI and Puerto Rico are not eligible) and who are 13 years and older at the time of enrollment.
  • Why do I have to be 13 to register?
    Children under the age of 13 are unable to join and cannot provide us with personal information. Please review our Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Can my school, church, or other organization collect caps as a group?
    Yes! Club Jarritos® is open to everybody!!*
  • How does Club Jarritos work?
    Simply drink your favorite beverages, look under your cap for points and report your codes online. Once you accumulate points in your online account, you can start redeeming for cool prizes.
  • Where do I get codes?
    You’ll find codes beneath the caps of Jarritos, Jarritos Kids, Mineragua, Sidral Mundet and Sangria Señorial products.
  • When do my points expire?
    The Club Jarritos promotion will run through December 31, 2013.
  • What are the participating brands and how many points is each worth?
    Participating Brands: Jarritos®, Jarritos Kids™, Mineragua®, Sangria Señorial® & Sidral Mundet®.Sizes –
    • 1.5L 5 points
    • Cristal 1 or 2 points
    • All other presentations: 1 point.
